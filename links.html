  • stille-liebe.de
    stille liebe is a Düsseldorf-based project by two young recording producers dedicated to making forgotten music of the 19th century publicly available again. Driven by a passion for classical music and the preservation of musical culture, they produce and publish professional recordings — giving young artists in particular a platform to reach wider audiences.
