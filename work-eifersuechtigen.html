The Jealous Ones

Die Eifersüchtigen

Opera in three acts · Oper in drei Aufzügen

Libretto and Music by Joachim Raff, WoO. 54

Cast

  • Don Geronimo — Bass
  • Don Claudio — Bariton
  • Donna Rosa — Sopran
  • Don Giulio — Tenor
  • Donna Bianca — Sopran
  • Beppino — Bass
  • Ninetta — Sopran

No choir.

Orchestra

2 flutes, 2 oboes, 2 clarinets, 2 bassoons, 2 horns, 2 trumpets, timpani, strings

Duration: 2h 15m

Editions Available

  • 0118-W541 — Vocal score (printed or PDF download)
  • Full orchestral score and parts available for hire

The opera received its world premiere by the Opernkollektiv Zürich. The performances were recorded and a CD publication is available on Naxos.
