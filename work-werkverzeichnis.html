Raff Werkverzeichnis

The Music of Joachim Raff

Raff Werkverzeichnis — Illustrated Catalogue

This is the first catalogue of the complete music of Joachim Raff to appear after the Raff-Werkverzeichnis by Albert Schäfer in 1888. It contains approximately 100 more works by Raff than its 19th-century predecessor, and includes useful information about each piece: content, scoring, first editions, and first performances.

The new catalogue was created over many years by the renowned Raff specialist Mark Thomas, creator of the ultimate online resource on Joachim Raff: www.raff.org.

Dies ist das erste komplette Verzeichnis der Werke Joachim Raffs seit dem Jahr 1888. Im Vergleich mit dem alten Werkverzeichnis von Albert Schäfer sind hier ca. 100 neu identifizierte Kompositionen aufgelistet.

The main language of the catalogue is English. Original citations are presented in German and English.
